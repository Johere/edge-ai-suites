# Hourly Fridge Recipe Generator

## Recipe 1: Protein-Packed Chicken & Egg Bowl (Based on current fridge)

**Ingredients:**
- 1 chicken breast (sliced)
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder

**Instructions:**
1. Sauté chicken breast in a pan until golden (5-7 mins)
2. Beat 2 eggs with milk, pour over chicken
3. Cook until eggs set (3-4 mins)
4. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder
5. Serve hot

**Nutrition:** ~350 calories, 45g protein, 15g fat
**Weight Loss Tip:** Skip the red velvet cake today - you've got better options!

## Recipe 2: Quick Egg Scramble with Milk

**Ingredients:**
- 3 eggs
- 2 tbsp milk
- Salt, pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Whisk eggs with milk
2. Scramble in pan over medium heat (4-5 mins)
3. Season to taste

**Nutrition:** ~250 calories, 18g protein
**Weight Loss Tip:** Add vegetables if you have any - increases volume without calories!

## Recipe 3: Light Milk & Egg Breakfast

**Ingredients:**
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- Pinch of cinnamon

**Instructions:**
1. Warm milk in pan
2. Add egg, cook gently
3. Sprinkle cinnamon

**Nutrition:** ~180 calories, 12g protein
**Weight Loss Tip:** Perfect low-calorie breakfast option!

---

## Recipe 4: Cake Alternative - Egg Muffin

**Ingredients:**
- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp milk
- Salt, pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Beat eggs with milk
2. Pour into muffin tin
3. Bake at 350°F for 15 mins

**Nutrition:** ~150 calories, 12g protein
**Weight Loss Tip:** Better than cake - high protein, low sugar!
